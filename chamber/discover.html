About Cyprus Chamber of Commerce and Industry

The Cyprus Chamber of Commerce and Industry (CCCI) is a private, financially independent organization founded in 1927 that represents the interests of Cypriot businesses to the government and promotes the country's economic development. It is a federation of local chambers in major cities, and its activities include advocating for members, providing business support services, and participating in EU programs like the Enterprise Europe Network.
